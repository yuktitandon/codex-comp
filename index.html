HOW IT WORKS

Simple to use. Sharp enough to feel real.

The product is designed to feel calm and obvious: one path for understanding the bad day, another for planning the next one more intelligently.

01

Tell the app about your day

Paste a messy log, a rough schedule, or a journal-style recap. No formatting required.

02

See where it actually went wrong

The app highlights the likely break point, time leaks, burnout pressure, and quantified drag.

03

Choose short or deep analysis

04

Plan tomorrow around real constraints

Set wake time, end time, commitments, and priorities, then turn that into a cleaner day shape.
